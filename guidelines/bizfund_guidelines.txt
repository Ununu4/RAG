# LENDER: bizfund

## funding_guidelines
Funding: $5,000 - $500,000
Term: 3 - 12 Months
Positions: 1st - 3rd
Payment Frequency: Daily & Weekly
Early Payoff Options (EP):
    30-Days: 15 Points Off Sell Rate (Example: 1.20 payback on 1.35, saving 15K on 100K funding)
    60-Days: 10 Points Off Sell Rate (Example: 1.25 payback on 1.35, saving 10K on 100K funding)
    90-Days: 5 Points Off Sell Rate (Example: 1.30 payback on 1.35, saving 5K on 100K funding)

## eligibility_criteria
Minimum Credit: 550+
Negative Days Per Month: <5
Federal Tax Liens: No Limitations
State Tax Liens: > $100K (May Need Coverage)
Ownership % Minimum: 50%
Time in Business: 1 year
BKs: Must Be Discharged or Dismissed
Minimum Deposit Volume: $20K/month
Minimum Avg. Daily Balances: $1K Minimum
Deposits: Minimum 3/month
Restricted Areas: Miami-Dade County, PR, LA
Auto Decline: 4 or more positions

Unique Industries Funded: Trucking ($60K min rev/month), Non-Profits, Gas Stations/Oil Related, Attorneys, Insurance Agencies, Staffing, Travel Agencies, Wireless Stores, CPAs, Places of Worship (case by case).

Prohibited Industries: Car Dealerships, Adult, Entertainment, Collection Agencies, Credit Bureaus, Publicly Traded Companies, Lenders, Gambling, Bail Bonds, Blood Banks, Pawn Shops, Nail Salons, Real Estate.

## deal_structure
Product Type: Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) indicated by "Sell Rate" and "Payback" terminology.
Offers: Hard Offers Upfront
Credit Pull: Soft Pulls Only
No COJs (Confessions of Judgment)
Customized Quoting
No State Restrictions (for general eligibility)
Stipulations Upfront: Signed Application, 3-Months Bank Statements
Stipulations Before Funding: Bank Verification, Drivers License, Voided Check, Merchant Interview
No Financials Required for deals < $350K
No Landlord Verification Needed

## key_findings
Funding available from $5,000 to $500,000 with terms ranging from 3 to 12 months, accepting positions up to 3rd.
Minimum credit score of 550+ is required, along with a minimum of 1 year in business.
Key financial requirements include a minimum of $20K/month in deposit volume, $1K average daily balances, and at least 3 deposits per month, with less than 5 negative days.
Crucially, no financials are required for deals under $350K, simplifying the submission process for smaller advances.
Offers are provided upfront based on soft pulls, and no Confessions of Judgment (COJs) are used.
Early payoff discounts are available at 30, 60, and 90 days.
A broad range of industries are considered, including specific unique niches, but a detailed list of prohibited industries is provided (e.g., adult, gambling, real estate, car dealerships). Restricted geographical areas are Miami-Dade County, PR, and LA.
