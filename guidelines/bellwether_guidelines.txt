# LENDER: bellwether

## funding_guidelines
- **Funding Range**: $20,000.00 - $200,000.00
- **Term Length**: 1 month (20 days) to 6 months (120 days)
- **Repayment**: Daily/Weekly
- **Early Pay Off Discounts**: Yes
- **Syndication Options**: Yes, up to 50%
- **Renewals**: Pays full commission

## eligibility_criteria
- **Time in Business**: 12 Months minimum
- **FICO**: Above 550
- **Positions**: 2nd positions +
- **Monthly Gross Deposits**: $40,000 (General), $200,000 (Trucking)
- **Deposit Frequency**: 5 Deposits minimum
- **Negative Days/NSFs**: Max 4 negative days per month, Overdraft protection preferred
- **Bankruptcies**: None within the last 5 years
- **Tax Liens**: Yes, if payment plan required
- **Judgments**: Yes, if satisfied within reasonable time frame
- **Rent Status**: Current
- **Hard Industry Restrictions**: Law, Auto, Real Estate, Non-profit
- **Preferred Industries**: Healthcare, Restaurants, Professional Services
- **Required Stipulations**: State or federal ID, Voided Check
- **Submission Requirements**: 4 months bank statements + Application
- **Month to Date**: Required after the 14th
- **Bank Verification**: Manual login, or decision logic

## deal_structure
- Funding available for businesses in 2nd position or higher.
- Short-term funding with duration ranging from 1 month (20 days) to 6 months (120 days).
- Repayments are structured on a daily or weekly basis.
- Early pay off discounts are offered.
- Syndication options are available for up to 50% of the deal.

## key_findings
- **Minimum Requirements**: 12 months in business, FICO above 550, and $40,000 in monthly gross deposits ($200,000 for trucking).
- **Funding Scope**: Offers $20,000 to $200,000 in funding with terms between 1 and 6 months, repaid daily or weekly.
- **Flexibility**: Accommodates 2nd position funding, up to 4 negative days/NSFs per month (overdraft protection preferred), tax liens (with payment plan), and satisfied judgments.
- **Industry Focus**: Avoids Law, Auto, Real Estate, and Non-profit industries; prefers Healthcare, Restaurants, and Professional Services.
- **Broker Benefits**: Pays full commission on renewals, offers early pay-off discounts, and allows syndication up to 50%.
