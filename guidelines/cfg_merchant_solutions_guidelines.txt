# LENDER: cfg_merchant_solutions

## funding_guidelines
Not specified.

## eligibility_criteria
*   **Revenue:** $10,000+ true monthly revenue (general); $50,000+ monthly revenue for 1st position trucking files.
*   **Time in Business (TIB):** 1 Year+
*   **Credit:** Credit score is not a factor.
*   **Bank Health:** Maximum 6 negative days in the most recent month.
*   **Industry Specific:** Trucking files must have factoring.

## deal_structure
*   **Position:** Funds 2nd, 3rd, and 4th position paper/files.
*   **Position (Trucking Exception):** Will consider 1st position for trucking files with $50,000+ monthly revenue and factoring.
*   **Exclusions:** No deals in a "reverse."

## key_findings
*   Funds 2nd, 3rd, and 4th position.
*   Minimum $10,000+ monthly true revenue.
*   TIB must be 1 Year+.
*   Credit score is disregarded.
*   Max 6 negative bank days in the most recent month.
*   Trucking deals require factoring, and can be 1st position if $50,000+ monthly revenue.
*   No "reverse" deals funded.
