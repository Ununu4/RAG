# LENDER: bitty_advance

## funding_guidelines
*   **New Funding:** $2,000 - $75,000
*   **Renewal Funding:** Up to $250,000
*   **Gold Product:** 130 payments, 1.49 factor, 12% commission, 20-35% remit.
*   **Silver Product:** 120 payments, 1.55 factor, 12% commission, 20-30% remit.
*   **Bronze Product:** 110 payments, 1.59 factor, 8% commission, 20-30% remit.
*   **Copper Product:** 80 payments, 1.59 factor, 8% commission, 18-30% remit.
*   **Upsell:** 4% minimum upsell required on Retention Pricing.

## eligibility_criteria
*   **Time In Business:**
    *   Gold: 12+ months.
    *   Silver, Bronze, Copper: 6+ months.
    *   Auto Decline: Less than 6 months.
*   **Credit Score (FICO):**
    *   Gold: 650+.
    *   Silver: 600+.
    *   Bronze: 551+.
    *   Copper: 450+.
    *   Auto Decline: FICO < 450.
*   **Revenue:**
    *   Gold: $40,000/month average.
    *   Silver, Bronze: $5,000/month average.
    *   Copper: $10,000/month average.
*   **Business Checking:** Required.
*   **Business Location/Website:** Required. Home-based applicants need an online presence.
*   **UCCs/Judgments:** No unresolved cash advance UCCs or business funding Judgments.
*   **Negative Days (Auto Decline):** 7+ in recent month, 14+ in last 2 months, 21+ in previous 3 months.

## deal_structure
*   Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) model with specified payments, factor rates, and remit percentages, structured into Gold, Silver, Bronze, and Copper product tiers.

## key_findings
*   Offers MCA products with funding from $2,000 up to $75,000 for new clients and up to $250,000 for renewals.
*   Minimum eligibility includes 6+ months TIB and FICO 450+ (with higher requirements for larger advances).
*   Monthly revenue requirements range from $5,000 to $40,000 depending on the product.
*   Strict auto-decline criteria based on TIB, FICO score, and negative bank days.
*   Requires a business checking account, a physical or demonstrable online business presence, and no unresolved UCCs or judgments.
*   Provides clear lists of Preferred, Standard, and Prohibited industries.
