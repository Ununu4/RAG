# LENDER: aspire_funding_platform

## funding_guidelines
Funding amounts range from $10,000 to $1,000,000. Terms are 3-12 months. Factor rates start from 1.30. Max Upsell is 12%. Remittance is on a Daily or Weekly basis.

## eligibility_criteria
Net of Financing Proceeds: 30% - 50% net required depending on risk level. Positions: 1st through 6th for Most Industries. Restricted States: New York. Sole Proprietor Restriction: California (Can fund in Cali as long as it's not a sole proprietor). Restricted Industries: Check Cashing, Financial Services, and Online Gambling. Industries We Love: Restaurants, Medical, Law Offices, Manufacturing. Industries we Prefer to See a Payment History With: Auto sales, Trucking, Solar, Roofing.

## deal_structure
This program provides Merchant Cash Advances (MCAs). Deals are structured with terms of 3-12 months and factor rates starting from 1.30. Maximum upsell is 12%. Remittance is collected daily or weekly. The program can fund positions from 1st through 6th.

## key_findings
This lender offers Merchant Cash Advances (MCAs) from $10,000 to $1,000,000 with terms of 3-12 months and factor rates starting at 1.30. They can fund up to 6th position. A net of 30%-50% financing proceeds is required. New York is a restricted state, and California only qualifies non-sole proprietors. Restricted industries include Check Cashing, Financial Services, and Online Gambling. They prefer industries like Restaurants, Medical, Law Offices, Manufacturing, and for Auto Sales, Trucking, Solar, and Roofing, a payment history is preferred. Required documentation includes the last three months' bank statements, details on any outstanding cash advances, and a signed application with credit authorization.
