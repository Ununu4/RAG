# LENDER: avanza

## funding_guidelines
- **Factor Rates:** Starting at 1.40.
- **Broker Commission/Points:** 16 points up-sell.
- **Net Funding:** Client must net 50% of the advance after buyouts.

## eligibility_criteria
- **Monthly Deposits:** Minimum $50,000 ($100,000 for Construction and some Utah merchants).
- **Time in Business:** Minimum 1 year (3 years for Construction).
- **Deposit Frequency:** Minimum 8 deposits per month.
- **Geographic Restrictions:** No California; some Utah with $100k/month minimum.
- **Prohibited Industries:** Attorneys, Trucking (due to economy), Auto Sales, Vape Shops.
- **Accepted Industries:** Marijuana businesses.
- **Credit History:** No defaults, even if paid off (except medical/healthcare).
- **Application Process:** No credit pull to provide an offer.

## deal_structure
- **Product Type:** Merchant Cash Advance (implied).
- **Positioning:** Will buyout up to 2 existing positions to take 1st position.

## key_findings
- **Minimum Monthly Deposits:** Generally $50,000, but $100,000 for Construction and certain Utah businesses.
- **Minimum Time in Business:** 1 year (3 years for Construction).
- **Strict Default Policy:** No past defaults accepted, even if paid off, with the sole exception of medical/healthcare defaults.
- **Funds Marijuana:** Explicitly funds marijuana businesses.
- **Broker Compensation:** 16 points up-sell is indicated.
- **Application Documents:** Voided check, Driver's license.
- **Funding Documents:** Proof of ownership (with percentage), 1 month Merchant Statement (if applicable), A/R report (if applicable).
- **No Credit Pull:** Offers can be provided without a credit pull.
