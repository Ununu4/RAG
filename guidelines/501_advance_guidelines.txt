# LENDER: 501_advance

## funding_guidelines
*   **Commissions:** 12%-14%, with monthly bonuses available.
*   **Terms:** 12 Months with Daily or weekly payments.
*   **Daily Ledgers Requirement:** $1,000.

## eligibility_criteria
*   **Minimum Monthly Revenue:** $20,000.
*   **Minimum Monthly Revenue (Construction):** $50,000.
*   **FICO Score:** 550+.
*   **Time in Business (TIB):** 1 Year.
*   **Negative Bank Days (NSFs/Negs):** Maximum 4.
*   **Bank Deposits:** Minimum 4 per month.
*   **Defaults:** No Defaults allowed.
*   **Restricted States:** California (CA), Hawaii (HI), Puerto Rico (PR).
*   **Prohibited Industries:** Commission based revenue, Auto Sales, Financial Firms, Transportation.

## deal_structure
*   **Position:** 2nd Position+.
*   **Payment Structure:** Daily or weekly payments.
*   **Term Length:** 12 Months.
*   **Buyouts/Payoffs:** Available for qualified merchants.

## key_findings
*   **Target Market:** Merchants requiring 2nd position or higher funding, including options for buyouts/payoffs.
*   **Key Minimums:** $20,000 monthly revenue ($50,000 for construction), FICO 550+, 1 year TIB.
*   **Bank Health:** Max 4 negative bank days, min 4 monthly deposits, and no prior defaults.
*   **Exclusions:** Funding is restricted in CA, HI, PR, and prohibited for auto sales, financial firms, transportation, and commission-based revenue industries.
*   **Broker Compensation:** Attractive commissions of 12-14% with additional monthly bonuses.
