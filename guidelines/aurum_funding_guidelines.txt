# LENDER: aurum_funding

Funding amounts: $10,000 to $1,000,000. Term lengths: 40 days to 180 days. Average fee range: 7%-10%. Cleaner files range: 3%-5%. Early pay discount available.

## eligibility_criteria
Minimum 1 year in business. Minimum 500+ credit score. Position: 2nd-10th. Minimum 5 deposits per month. Minimum $30,000 monthly revenue. Less than 5 negative days. Less than 5 NSF occurrences. Excluded industries: Staffing, Check Cashing/ATM, Auto Sales, Non-Profits, Real Estate. Trucking is considered with good pay history.

Likely a Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) or similar alternative funding product, indicated by "position," "neg days," "NSF," and "fee range." Terms are 40-180 days. Deals are structured with a fee-based model, and an early pay discount can be offered to close deals.
