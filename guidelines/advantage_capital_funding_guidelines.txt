# LENDER: advantage_capital_funding

## funding_guidelines
- **Minimum Advance Amount:** $10,000
- **Maximum Advance Amount:** $2,000,000
- **Term Range (Standard):** 4-10 Months (80-160 days)
- **Term Range (Higher Tier):** Up to 10 months / 40 weeks
- **Factor Rate (Standard):** 1.49
- **Pricing (Higher Tier):** 1.29 – 1.39
- **Maximum Holdback (Standard):** 25%
- **Holdback (Higher Tier):** 10-14%
- **Payments:** Daily and Weekly
- **Commission:** 12% on factor rate of 1.49

## eligibility_criteria
- **Minimum Time In Business (TIB):** 1 year (standard), 3 years (Higher Tier)
- **Minimum Average Gross Sales (AGS):** $350,000
- **Minimum FICO:** 550 (standard), 650+ (Higher Tier)
- **Ownership Required:** 51%
- **Bankruptcy Discharge:** At least 1 year prior
- **Minimum Open Tradelines:** 3
- **Positions:** 1st through 4th (standard), 1st to 2nd position only (Higher Tier)
- **Geographic Coverage:** Accepting Submissions in all 50 states

**Exclusions (Prohibited Industries & Auto-Declines):**
- Adult Entertainment, Attorneys/Law Firms, Auto Dealerships, Class 3 Weapon Dealers, Collection Agencies, Credit Repair Companies, Entertainment & Event Ticket Sales, Kiosks, Financial Products Companies, Gas Stations, Telemarketers, Non-for-Profit Companies, Mortgage Lenders, Trucking / Hauling, Multi-Level Marketing, Diamond / Jewelry Sales, Pawn Shops, Publicly Traded Companies, Real Estate brokers, Detective Services, Title Loans, Travel Agencies, Online Home Based, Bail Bond Companies, Auto Content Websites, Auction Companies.
- **Additional Auto-Declines:** Non-Profit Organizations, Multiple Bankruptcies, Trucking Industry, Prior MCA Defaults.

## deal_structure
- The structure is based on Merchant Cash Advances (MCA) as indicated by "Factor rate," "holdback," and "Daily and Weekly payments."
- **Standard Approvals:** 80-160-day Terms, Factor rate of 1.49, Maximum 25% holdback.
- **Higher Tier Approvals:** Max funding at 10 month/40-week term, Pricing 1.29 – 1.39, 10-14% holdback.
- **Positions:** 1st to 4th for standard deals, 1st to 2nd only for higher tier deals.
- **Commissions:** 12% paid on a factor rate of 1.49.

## key_findings
- **Two Tiers of Funding:** Standard deals (550 FICO, 1-year TIB, 1.49 factor, up to 4th position) and Higher Tier deals (650+ FICO, 3-year TIB, 1.29-1.39 pricing, 1st-2nd position only).
- **Comprehensive Prohibited Industries List:** A wide range of specific industries are explicitly excluded, including non-profits, trucking, and those with prior MCA defaults or multiple bankruptcies.
- **Documentation Requirements:**
    - **Submission:** Signed application (51% ownership, last 30 days), 3 months complete bank statements (4 months for certain states).
    - **Funding:** Valid Driver's License, Business Voided Check, Site Inspection (Virtual for standard, physical for Higher Tier), Proof of Ownership, Bank Verification, Funding Call.
    - **Additional for $100,000+:** Tax Returns, Business Financials (P&L/Balance Sheet).
    - **Higher Tier Stips:** Most Recent Tax Return, Financials (P&L/Balance Sheet), AR/AP Aging, Manual Bank Login, Site Inspection.
- **Minimums:** $10k advance, $350k AGS, 550 FICO, 1 year TIB.
