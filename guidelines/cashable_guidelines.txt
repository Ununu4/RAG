# LENDER: cashable

## funding_guidelines
- **Funding Amounts:** $10,000.00 - $500,000.00
- **Max Term:** 120 days (24 weeks)
- **Commission Structure:** Up to 15 points
- **Payment Frequency:** Daily & Weekly payments available

## eligibility_criteria
- **Positions:** 2nd - 10th
- **Minimum Monthly Revenue:** $25,000.00
- **Minimum Monthly Deposits:** 5 Deposits
- **Credit Requirements:** No Credit Checks, No FICO Requirement, No DataMerch search
- **Time in Business:** 1 Year
- **NSF Limit:** Max 5 NSF's Monthly
- **Previous Defaults:** Accepted if positive payment history after initial default, and defaults are not within the last 30 days.
- **Aggressive offers:** Available on high-leveraged files (pending receivables).
- **"HOP" offers:** Available on a case-by-case basis (Max term 60 days).
- **Reverses in place:** Can fund with reverses in place (Reverse Docs must be submitted for review).
- **Restricted Industries/Locations:**
    - HAWAII/ALASKA/PUERTO RICO/CANADA
    - AUTO SALES WITH NO AUTO REPAIR SERVICES
    - FINANCIAL SERVICES
    - NON-PROFITS
    - ALL CASH/ZELLE DEPOSITS
    - 1 TRUCK TRANSPORTATION/TRUCKING
    - CONSULTING/MARKETING (Case by case)
    - VISA/PASSPORT SERVICES (Restricted)
    - LAW FIRMS (Case by case)

## deal_structure
- Offers Syndication opportunities.
- Funds previous defaults under specific conditions.
- Provides "HOP" offers with a maximum term of 60 days.
- Can fund with reverses in place upon submission and review of documentation.
- Offers daily and weekly payment options.
- Incremental funding and monthly bonuses available.

## key_findings
- **Flexible Eligibility:** Accepts positions from 2nd to 10th, requires a minimum of $25,000 monthly revenue and 5 deposits, and only 1 year in business.
- **Credit Forgiving:** No credit checks, no FICO requirement, and no DataMerch search. Also funds previous defaults under specific conditions (positive payment history after initial default, not within the last 30 days).
- **Diverse Offerings:** Provides aggressive offers on high-leveraged files, "HOP" offers (max 60 days), and can fund with reverses in place.
- **Broker Friendly:** Offers up to 15 points commission, syndication opportunities, and highlights incremental funding and monthly bonuses.
- **Submission Requirements:** Requires a signed & dated application (within 30 days), 3 months business banking statements (PDF), MTD if submitted past the 15th of the month (PDF), payback months for revenue drops (PDF), and indication of any open advance balances.
