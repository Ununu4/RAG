# LENDER: alternative_funding_group

## funding_guidelines
- **Standard Program:**
    - Funding Amount: Up to $1 Million
    - Term: 3-12 months
    - Commission: Up to 14 points
    - Position: 1st - 4th
    - Payment Frequency: Daily and Weekly
    - Same Day Funding: Available
    - Pre-Payment Discounts: Available
- **Reverse Consolidation:**
    - Max Program Term: Up to 200 days
    - Commission: Up to 14 points
    - Fixed Buy Rate: 1.32
    - Fixed Sell Rate: 1.46
    - Payment Reduction: Typically lowers payments by 40-60%.
    - Additional Advance: Often nets clients an additional advance.
- **Premium Pricing Program:**
    - Max Program Term: Up to 100 days
    - Commission: Up to 10 points
    - Fixed Buy Rate: 1.21
    - Fixed Sell Rate: 1.30 (includes 6% fees)

## eligibility_criteria
- **General (Standard Program):**
    - Time in Business (TIB): Minimum 1 year
    - FICO Score: Minimum 550 (Experian)
    - Monthly Average Revenue: Minimum $30k
    - Average Daily Balance: Minimum $1K+
    - Business Checking Account: Required
    - Ownership: 51% ownership on contract signage
- **General (Reverse Consolidation & Premium Pricing):**
    - Bank Statements: 3 months plus
    - MTD Max Negative Days: 4 days per month
- **Specific Program Requirements:**
    - Reverse Consolidation: Minimum TIB 24 months
    - Premium Pricing Program: Minimum TIB 12 months
- **Industry Specific:**
    - **Preferred Industries:** Retail, Restaurant, Medical, Cannabis, Manufacturing, Attorneys/Law Offices, Auto Sales.
    - **Specific Preferred Industries with notes:** Financial Consultants (Stock/Crypto brokers/traders are restricted), Real Estate (Fix and Flip, Buying/Selling, Management/Construction accepted).
    - **Industry Limitations (Standard Program):**
        - Construction: Minimum Revenue $75k / Minimum TIB 3 years
        - Trucking: Minimum Revenue $75k / Minimum TIB 3 years
    - **Restricted for Reverse Consolidation Program:** Construction and Logistics.
    - **Restricted Industries (Overall):** Stock/Crypto brokers or Traders, Cash Checking, Bail Bonds.
- **Geographical Restrictions:**
    - Restricted States: California & New York.

## deal_structure
- **Loan Type:** Merchant Cash Advance (MCA)
- **Credit Check:** Soft Credit Pull Only
- **Contracting:** E-Signed Contract (or Hard Copy at Client Request)
- **Payment Processing:**
    - Weekly fundings: Debited on Friday following closing date.
    - Daily fundings: Debit initiated on the next business day.
- **Underwriting Process:**
    1. Application and risk evaluation.
    2. Offer generation and delivery to ISO.
    3. Contract signing and final steps review.
    4. Stipulation review, merchant interview, bank login.
    5. Final review and funding call.
    6. Commissions paid to partners the following Friday.
- **Renewals/Add-ons:** Merchants eligible at 50% paid down. Partners receive commission on the full renewal amount.
- **Syndication:** Allowed on deals.
- **Required Documents (Submission):** Last 3 months of Bank statements, Signed application.
- **Required Documents (Contract Request):** Merchant Best Contact Number and Email, License or Government Issued ID, Voided Check.
- **Bank Verification:** Manual Log in for Bank Verification required.
- **Contact for Submissions:** uw@altfunding.com (cc ISO Manager)
- **Contact for Contract Requests:** contracts@altfunding.com (cc ISO Manager)

## key_findings
- **Diverse Programs:** Offers Standard MCA (3-12 months, up to $1M), Reverse Consolidation (up to 200 days), and Premium Pricing (up to 100 days) to suit various merchant needs.
- **Accessible Eligibility:** Minimum FICO 550, 1 year TIB, and $30k monthly revenue provide broad accessibility for standard programs.
- **Industry Nuances:** Construction and Trucking require higher TIB/revenue for standard programs and are restricted from Reverse Consolidations. Certain industries (e.g., Stock/Crypto brokers, Cash Checking, Bail Bonds) and states (CA, NY) are restricted.
- **Strong Broker Support:** Features include soft credit pull, syndication allowance, weekly commission payouts, and a transparent underwriting process with dedicated support.
- **Expedited Funding:** Same Day Funding is available for quick access to capital.
